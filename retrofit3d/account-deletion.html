Delete your Retrofit Factory 3D account & data

App: Retrofit Factory 3D (io.github.tlaba.retrofit3d) · Developer contact: legops@gmail.com

Cloud-save accounts are optional in Retrofit Factory 3D. If you created one and want it gone, this page explains exactly how, and what gets deleted.

How to request deletion

  1. Email legops@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Retrofit Factory 3D account".
  2. Send it from the email address you signed in with (that's how we verify it's you).
  3. We delete the account and confirm back to you within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email address and user ID)
  • All cloud game saves linked to the account
  • Any content-pack entitlements linked to the account

Deletion is permanent. Nothing is retained afterwards — there is no marketing database, analytics profile, or backup copy kept beyond the provider's routine short-term backups, which expire automatically.

What you can do without contacting us

  • Delete a plant's cloud save: starting a New Game on that plant replaces its cloud save; signing out stops all syncing.
  • Delete all local data: uninstall the app, or clear its storage in Android settings. On-device progress is removed immediately.
