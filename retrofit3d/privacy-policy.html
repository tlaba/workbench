Privacy Policy — Retrofit Factory 3D

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Short version: the game works fully offline, your progress lives on your device, and the only personal data we ever store is your email address — and only if you choose to turn on cloud saves. No ads, no analytics, no tracking, ever.

What this policy covers

This policy covers the Retrofit Factory 3D Android app and the web version it wraps (hosted at tlaba.github.io/workbench/retrofit3d/). Retrofit Factory 3D is made by an independent developer ("Retrofit 3D").

Data we store on your device

Your game progress is saved on your device, in the browser's local storage. It stays there. It is not sent anywhere unless you turn on cloud saves. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this local save is deleted.

Optional cloud saves

Cloud saves are off by default. The game is fully playable without them.

If you choose to sign in — with a one-time email code or with Google sign-in — we store the following on Supabase, a hosted database provider:

  • Your email address
  • A user ID
  • Your game-save data
  • If you redeem a pack key: which content packs your account owns

Why: so your progress can follow you across devices and survive a reinstall. That is the only reason.

How it's protected: data travels over HTTPS. The database uses row-level security, which means only the signed-in player can access their own rows.

What we do NOT collect

  • No ads
  • No analytics
  • No tracking SDKs
  • No location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers
  • No selling or sharing of your data
  • No use of your data for advertising

If you never sign in, we hold no account data about you at all.

Notifications

The game can show optional notifications, like "machine breakdown" or "day complete." These are generated locally on your device. There is no push server, and nothing is sent off your device to make them work. You can turn them off in your device's notification settings.

Children

Retrofit Factory 3D is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created a cloud-save account, email us and we will delete it.

Data deletion and your choices

  • Local data: clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Your on-device save is gone.
  • Cloud data: email us at legops@gmail.com and ask us to delete your account. We will delete your account and all associated data (email address, user ID, game saves, pack entitlements) within 30 days. Details: account deletion.
  • You can also simply stop using cloud saves at any time and keep playing offline.

Third-party infrastructure

We use three outside services, only for the purposes described here:

  • GitHub Pages hosts the game's files. Like any web host, it may transiently process standard hosting logs, which can include IP addresses.
  • Supabase stores cloud-save data (only if you sign in). As an infrastructure provider, it may also transiently process standard hosting logs, including IP addresses.
  • Google sign-in is one of the two optional ways to sign in. Google handles the sign-in step under its own privacy policy; we receive your email address from it.

We do not receive or keep these hosting logs ourselves, and we do not use any of these services to track you.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update it at this same location and change the effective date at the top. If a change meaningfully affects how cloud-save data is handled, we will say so clearly.
